01.About Me

I’m Abbas Manning, a 19-year-old Front-End Web Developer from the UK, with a story rooted in curiosity, perseverance, and a deep passion for creating digital experiences.


When I first set out on my web development journey, I dreamed of becoming a Full-Stack Developer. I was eager and ambitious, trying to learn everything all at once front-end, back-end, databases, and more. I threw myself into every corner of development, but as I dove deeper, I hit a wall.


I found myself understanding concepts but when it came to writing code from scratch, I struggled. It was frustrating, but instead of giving up, I decided to take a step back, reassess, and narrow my focus on one area where I could truly excel: Front-End development (Shopify Development). This choice wasn’t just about simplifying my path it was about mastering a craft. I wanted to take the time to truly understand how to build intuitive, user-friendly websites.


Fast forward three years, and I’ve transformed from a total beginner into a confident Junior Front-End Developer. Along the way, I’ve fallen in love with the process of turning creative ideas into functional, beautiful digital experiences. Building websites isn’t just a job for me; it’s a passion. There’s something deeply satisfying about crafting an interface that not only looks great but is intuitive and enjoyable for users.


Every line of code I write, every design I bring to life has been a stepping stone, helping me grow into the developer I am today.
